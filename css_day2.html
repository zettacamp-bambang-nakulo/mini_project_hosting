Hero Indonesia

A hero is a person who stands out for his courage and sacrifice in defending the truth, or a valiant warrior.

National heroes are given to fighters who have contributed to the Republic of Indonesia, fought for the State of Indonesia, and won the independence of the Republic of Indonesia.

Kapitan Pattimura

Pattimura is a national hero from Maluku who acts as a warlord in the resistance of the Maluku people with the Dutch VOC army.

Soekarno

Ir. Soekarno who came from Surabaya. Ir. Soekarno was the first president of Indonesia who also played a role as the originator of the basic Pancasila state that we use today.

Mohammad Hatta

Bung Hatta's spirit of patriotism had grown since he was young with his participation as an activist, member of a national movement organization to become a statesman who accompanied Soekarno to fight for Indonesia from Dutch colonialism.

Tan Malaka

His thoughts, which are contained in various writings and have inspired Soekarno, Hatta, Sjahrir and other national movement figures to fight for the independence of the Indonesian nation from the invaders.
